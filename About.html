About Mighty Solutions

Mighty Solutions is a dynamic company specializing in Electronic Security Surveillance, IT Sales & Services, and Fire Hydrant Systems. With a strong focus on Electronic Security Surveillance and IT Services, we are committed to delivering top-notch solutions to our clients in the most efficient manner possible.

Our services encompass a wide range of IT solutions, including comprehensive and non-comprehensive FMS/AMC for IT equipment, server and desktop management, network management, and e-mail and database server management. We also offer IT outsourcing, security, and backup management services to ensure the smooth functioning of your IT infrastructure.

In the realm of Electronic Security and Surveillance, we provide cutting-edge solutions such as electronic security cameras, wireless security systems, access control security systems, and CCTV dome cameras. Our team is skilled in installing and maintaining fire hydrant systems, fire alarm panels, intrusion alarm panels, and other security equipment to keep your premises safe and secure. Moreover, we offer time and attendance management solutions to streamline workforce management and LED lighting solutions for commercial use, promoting energy efficiency. Our accomplishments are reflected in our esteemed clientele, including well-known companies like Good Earth Pvt Ltd (Eicher Group), NICOBAR, KFC – Sapphire Foods India Pvt. Ltd., and many more. We take pride in providing exceptional technical support and ensuring customer satisfaction.
